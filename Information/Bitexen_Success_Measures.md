# Bitexen SA Launch - Success Measures and KPIs

## Campaign Performance Framework

**Prepared by:** Broadbrand (Digital Solutions Group)
**Date:** March 2026
**Reference:** Bitexen SA Launch Proposal + Annexure A (Media Plan)

This document defines the key performance indicators (KPIs), measurement methodology, and benchmark targets for the Bitexen South Africa launch campaign. All benchmarks are drawn from global fintech, crypto exchange, and digital advertising industry standards for emerging market launches.

---

## 1. Brand Awareness

Brand awareness is the primary objective of the launch phase. The campaign must establish Bitexen as a recognised and trusted name in the South African crypto market within the first 12 weeks.

### KPIs

| Metric | Target | Benchmark Source |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Aided brand awareness (post-campaign survey) | 25-30% among target audience | Fintech launch benchmarks in emerging markets typically reach 20-35% aided awareness within first quarter. Luno achieved ~30% aided awareness in SA within first year. |
| Unaided brand recall | 8-12% among target audience | Global average for new fintech brands at 90 days is 5-10%. Target slightly above due to heavy TV + OOH weight. |
| Total media impressions (all channels) | 80-100 million | Combined vendor media and digital performance across 12-week flight. DStv On-Target packages alone deliver 4M+ guaranteed views. |
| Video views (completed) | 10-15 million | Across DStv Stream, YouTube, TikTok, Meta, Viu, SABC+, and programmatic video. |
| OOH reach (estimated) | 2-3 million unique exposures | Polygon digital billboard and transit network across Gauteng and KZN metros. |
| Radio reach (cumulative) | 3-4 million listeners | ECR + Jacaranda FM combined audience across 2-week drive-time flights. |

### Measurement Approach

Brand awareness targets are estimated based on media weight, channel reach, and industry benchmarks. Digital proxy metrics (search volume uplift, social mention growth, direct website traffic) will be used as ongoing indicators of awareness growth.

**Optional add-on:** A formal brand tracking survey (pre/post campaign, 1,000+ respondents across Gauteng, KZN, and Western Cape) can be commissioned separately to validate aided/unaided awareness, brand favourability, and message recall with statistical confidence. Estimated cost: R85,000-R150,000 depending on sample size and methodology. This is not included in the campaign budget but is recommended for clients who require research-grade brand measurement.

## 2. Digital Engagement

Engagement measures how actively the target audience interacts with Bitexen content across digital touchpoints. The primary digital engagement budget is the Paid Social allocation (R567,000 across Meta and TikTok), supplemented by Programmatic (R315,000), Google/YouTube (R252,000), and Influencer partnerships (R189,000).

### KPIs

| Metric | Target | Budget Basis | Benchmark Source |
|--------|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| Social media followers (combined Meta + TikTok) | 50,000-75,000 | R567,000 Paid Social + R189,000 Influencer | Crypto exchanges in new markets typically acquire 30k-80k followers in launch quarter. |
| Social engagement rate | 3.5-5.0% | R567,000 Paid Social | Global fintech Instagram average 1.5-3.0%. Crypto brands 3.0-5.0%. TikTok average 4.0-6.0%. |
| Video completion rate (15s/30s) | 65-75% | R315,000 Programmatic Video + R252,000 YouTube | Industry benchmark: 15s = 70-80%; 30s = 55-65%. Crypto/finance content performs 5-10% above average. |
| Click-through rate (display) | 0.35-0.50% | R175,000 Mediamark Display + R165,000 Programmatic Display | Global display CTR average 0.35%. SA fintech display 0.40-0.55% (Mediamark benchmark). |
| Click-through rate (video) | 0.80-1.20% | R100,000 Mediamark Video + R150,000 Programmatic Video | Global video CTR average 0.80%. Sports content pre-roll typically 1.0-1.5%. |
| Click-through rate (paid social) | 1.20-1.80% | R567,000 Paid Social | Meta fintech average 1.10%. TikTok average 1.50-2.00% for financial services. |
| OnsideZA episode engagement | 8+ min avg view duration | R345,000 OnsideZA | Baseline AVD 8.14 min. Branded episodes should maintain or exceed. Target 98% like-to-dislike ratio. |
| #Promoted fan card tool completions | 25,000-40,000 | R200,000 production (non-working) + social amplification | Successful gamified fintech tools generate 20k-50k completions in launch quarter. |
| Website sessions (bitexen.co.za) | 200,000-350,000 | R126,000 Content/SEO + R120,000 Google Search + all paid channels | Crypto exchange launch sites generate 150k-400k sessions in first quarter depending on media weight. |

### Measurement Approach

Real-time dashboards via Meta Business Suite, TikTok Ads Manager, Google Analytics 4, and DV360 reporting. Weekly performance reports with optimisation recommendations. OnsideZA provides YouTube Analytics data per episode. Website analytics via GA4 with UTM tracking across all paid channels.

## 3. App Performance

App downloads and early user activation are the bridge between awareness and revenue. Direct app install campaigns are funded through the Performance / App Install allocation (R126,000), with additional installs driven as a secondary outcome from Paid Social (R567,000), Google/YouTube (R252,000), and the vendor media placements.

### KPIs

| Metric | Target | Budget Basis | Benchmark Source |
|--------|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| App downloads (direct install campaigns) | 5,000-8,400 | R126,000 at R15-R25 CPI (Google UAC R60k + Meta App Install R66k) | SA fintech CPI ranges R15-R35. Google UAC typically delivers lowest CPI; Meta slightly higher but better quality. |
| App downloads (assisted, all channels) | 45,000-72,000 | Driven by Paid Social, Search, DStv, Radio, OnsideZA as secondary conversion | Crypto exchange launches in emerging markets achieve 40k-100k downloads in first quarter. Luno SA hit ~100k within first 6 months. |
| App downloads (total, 12 weeks) | 50,000-80,000 | Total across direct + assisted channels | Combined direct install spend + halo effect from R4.5M total media investment |
| Blended cost per install (CPI) | R15-R30 | Direct install channels only | Global fintech CPI averages: Android R12-R25, iOS R20-R40. |
| Effective CPI (all media) | R56-R90 | Total media spend / total installs | Full-funnel CPI accounting for awareness-driven installs. Comparable crypto launches see R50-R120 effective CPI. |
| App store rating | 4.0+ stars | N/A (product quality) | Crypto apps below 3.5 stars see significantly higher churn. Luno SA: 4.2; VALR: 4.4. |
| Day 1 retention | 35-45% | N/A (product quality) | Global fintech average 25-35%. Crypto apps with strong onboarding achieve 35-45%. |
| Day 7 retention | 18-25% | N/A (product quality) | Global fintech D7 average 15-20%. Top crypto apps achieve 20-28%. |
| Day 30 retention | 10-15% | N/A (product quality) | Global fintech D30 average 8-12%. Binance/Coinbase benchmark 12-18% in mature markets. |

### Measurement Approach

App install attribution via Google UAC and Meta SDK. Retention cohort analysis via Firebase or internal Bitexen analytics. CPI tracked per channel and per creative variant. Weekly CPI optimisation with underperforming channels reallocated to top performers.

## 4. User Acquisition and Activation

Beyond downloads, the campaign must drive meaningful user activation: account creation, KYC completion, and first transaction. These metrics determine ROI against the total media investment of R4,514,430.

### KPIs

| Metric | Target | Budget Basis | Benchmark Source |
|--------|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| Registered accounts (KYC started) | 35,000-55,000 | 65-75% of 50-80k downloads | Conversion from download to registration in crypto apps is typically 60-75%. |
| KYC completed | 20,000-35,000 | 55-65% of registrations | SA fintech KYC completion rates range 50-70%. Friction: ID upload, selfie, proof of address. |
| First deposit made | 10,000-18,000 | 50-55% of KYC complete | Crypto exchange benchmark. |
| First trade executed | 8,000-15,000 | 80-85% of depositors | Most users who deposit will trade. |
| Cost per acquired customer (CPA) | R300-R565 | R4,514,430 total media / 8k-15k traders | Full-funnel CPA against total media investment. Global crypto exchange CPA ranges R100-R500 in mature markets. Higher for new-market launches. |
| CPA (direct performance only) | R84-R197 | R1,575,000 digital performance / 8k-15k traders | CPA against digital performance spend only. SA fintech CPAs average R180-R350. |
| Fan token specific: first fan token purchase | 3,000-6,000 | 25-40% of active traders | Unique Bitexen differentiator. No direct SA benchmark; estimated from global fan token adoption rates. |

### Funnel Conversion Benchmarks (Budget-Linked)

| Stage | Volume | Conversion Rate | Driving Budget |
|-------|--------|----------------|---------------|
| Impressions | 80-100M | - | R4,514,430 total media |
| Website visits | 200-350k | 0.25-0.35% of impressions | R252,000 Google Search + R315,000 Programmatic + R126,000 Content/SEO |
| App downloads | 50-80k | 15-25% of web visits | R126,000 App Install + R567,000 Paid Social (assisted) |
| Registration started | 35-55k | 65-75% of downloads | Product UX / onboarding quality |
| KYC completed | 20-35k | 55-65% of registrations | Product UX / KYC flow optimisation |
| First deposit | 10-18k | 50-55% of KYC complete | Product trust signals, deposit flow |
| First trade | 8-15k | 80-85% of depositors | Product / market conditions |
| Fan token purchase | 3-6k | 25-40% of traders | R345,000 OnsideZA + Paid Social fan token creative |

### Measurement Approach

End-to-end funnel tracked via Bitexen's internal analytics platform. UTM parameters and attribution windows (7-day click, 1-day view) applied across all paid channels to attribute conversions back to specific media placements. Weekly funnel analysis with bottleneck identification and CRO recommendations.

## 5. Content and SEO

Content performance measures the effectiveness of the always-on education hub and organic search strategy.

### KPIs

| Metric | Target | Benchmark Source |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Organic search sessions (12 weeks) | 15,000-30,000 | New fintech sites with dedicated SEO investment typically achieve 10k-30k organic sessions in first quarter. |
| Keyword rankings (page 1) | 10-15 target keywords | Target keywords: "buy crypto SA", "fan tokens", "crypto exchange South Africa", "PSL fan tokens", "what are fan tokens". Competitive landscape includes Luno, VALR, Binance. |
| Content pieces published | 12 cornerstone articles | One per week across education hub. Topics mapped to search intent and funnel stage. |

| Content distribution CTR (Taboola/Outbrain) | 0.15-0.25% | Native content distribution benchmark for finance vertical is 0.12-0.20%. |

### Measurement Approach

Google Search Console for keyword tracking and organic performance. GA4 for on-site engagement metrics. Taboola/Outbrain dashboards for content distribution performance. Monthly SEO audit and content gap analysis.

## 6. Radio and Audio Performance

Audio-specific metrics for the Mediamark Audio campaign across ECR and Jacaranda FM.

### KPIs

| Metric | Target | Benchmark Source |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Estimated reach (2-week flight) | 2.5-3.5M listeners | ECR + Jacaranda FM combined weekly reach. Drive-time slots deliver highest reach concentration. |
| Frequency (average OTS) | 3-4x per listener | 24 total spots across 2 weeks, concentrated in drive-time. Sufficient for message recall without fatigue. |
| Prompted ad recall (post-campaign) | 20-30% | Radio ad recall benchmarks in SA range 15-35% depending on creative quality and frequency. Drive-time placement improves recall by 15-20% vs off-peak. |
| Digital audio companion CTR | 0.40-0.60% | Mediamark digital audio companion ads benchmark at 0.35-0.55% CTR. Audio + visual companion format lifts engagement vs audio-only. |
| Website traffic uplift (radio attribution) | 10-15% uplift during flight | Measured by comparing baseline website traffic to traffic during radio flight windows, controlling for other media. |

### Measurement Approach

BRC RAM data for reach and frequency estimates. Digital audio tracked via Mediamark reporting dashboard. Website traffic uplift measured by comparing baseline sessions to sessions during radio flight windows.

**Optional add-on:** SoundInsights FM Radio Attribution Kit (R150,000) provides granular on-air to online tracking with custom attribution modelling. SoundInsights Next Gen Neuro testing (R150,000) measures real attention and emotional impact of audio ads. Both available via Mediamark but not included in the campaign budget.

## 8. OnsideZA and Podcast Performance

Metrics specific to the OnsideZA 6-episode campaign and Outside Broadcast activation.

### KPIs

| Metric | Target | Benchmark Source |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Total views (6 episodes) | 180,000-250,000 | OnsideZA averages 117k views/month with base performance of 30k views per episode. Branded episodes with strong integration can see 20-40% uplift. |
| Average view duration (branded eps) | 7.5+ min | Baseline AVD is 8.14 min. Branded content typically sees 5-10% AVD reduction. Target is to maintain above 7.5 min, indicating the integration does not disrupt viewer engagement. |
| Like-to-dislike ratio | 97%+ | OnsideZA baseline is 98%. Branded content that feels authentic maintains this ratio. A drop below 95% signals audience rejection of the integration. |
| Description link clicks | 2,000-4,000 | YouTube description link CTR for sponsored content is typically 1-2% of views. |
| Outside Broadcast views | 50,000-80,000 | OB episodes tend to outperform studio episodes due to novelty and event context. Nigeria vs Bafana watchalong hit 176k views. |
| Social mentions / hashtag usage | 1,000-2,500 | Organic social mentions of #BitexenSA and related hashtags driven by OnsideZA audience during and after episodes. |

### Measurement Approach

YouTube Analytics data provided by OnsideZA per episode. Description link tracking via UTM. Social listening for hashtag and brand mention tracking via community management tools.
