## 4. OnsideZA (Sports Podcast / YouTube)

OnsideZA is South Africa's number one sports-focused podcast, averaging 117,000 views per month with 70 million total impressions. The audience is 86% male, with an average view duration of 8.14 minutes. The show is part of the wider Podcast and Chill Network (combined social influence of 11M+), which includes Podcast and Chill with MacG, Spreading Humours, and PNC (People Need Comedy).

OnsideZA is especially relevant given Bitexen's football fan token positioning. The show delivers the highest engagement during football match watchalongs among SA YouTube content creators.

## 7. Digital Performance Media

In addition to the vendor-specific media placements above, the campaign requires a dedicated digital performance layer to drive measurable acquisition, community growth, and conversion outcomes. This section maps to the Digital allocation in the main proposal (50% of working media = R1,575,000) and covers self-serve and programmatic channels that sit outside the vendor packages detailed in sections 1-6.

### 7.1 Paid Social (Meta + TikTok) - R567,000

| Platform | Allocation | Objective |
|----------|-----------|-----------|
| Meta (Instagram + Facebook) | R250,000 | Awareness and retargeting via lookalike audiences built from Bitexen's Turkish user base. Carousel and Reels formats featuring fan token content and "Starting XI + 1" creative. |
| TikTok | R317,000 | Short-form video for 18-34 male audience. Football challenge content, fan token explainers, creator duets. TikTok is the primary short-form driver for the Podcast and Chill Network audience. |

Paid social is the primary top-of-funnel awareness driver. Creative assets leverage the hero TVC cuts (30s, 15s) and fan card visuals produced in the non-working budget.

### 7.2 Programmatic Display and Video - R315,000

| Channel | Allocation | Deliverable |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| Programmatic Display (GDN + DV360) | R165,000 | Retargeting site visitors and app page viewers. Lookalike audience expansion based on crypto interest segments. Rich media units via Newsroom AI partnership. |
| Programmatic Video (YouTube + Open Exchange) | R150,000 | Pre-roll and mid-roll video across sports, finance, and entertainment content. 15s and 30s cuts from hero TVC. Frequency-capped to avoid fatigue. |

Programmatic buying enables real-time optimisation and audience-level targeting that complements the premium placement buys through DStv and Mediamark.

### 7.3 Google Search and YouTube - R252,000

| Channel | Allocation | Deliverable |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| Google Search (Brand + Category) | R120,000 | Brand protection (Bitexen, Bitexen SA) and category capture (buy crypto SA, fan tokens, crypto exchange South Africa). Defensive bidding against competitor terms. |
| YouTube (TrueView + Bumpers) | R132,000 | TrueView in-stream ads targeting sports and finance audiences. 6s bumper ads for frequency and recall. Tutorial and education content promotion. |

Search captures high-intent users actively looking for crypto solutions. YouTube extends video reach beyond the DStv and Mediamark ecosystems into Google's inventory.

### 7.4 Influencer and Creator Partnerships - R189,000

| Tier | Number | Per Creator | Total | Focus |
|------|--------|------------|-------|-------|
| Macro Influencers (100k-500k) | 3 | R35,000 | R105,000 | SA football commentators and sports culture creators. Authentic integration of Bitexen fan tokens into existing content formats. |
| Micro Influencers (10k-100k) | 6 | R14,000 | R84,000 | Crypto/fintech and football niche creators. App walkthrough content, fan token unboxing, match-day prediction content. |

Creator partnerships extend reach into communities that traditional and programmatic media cannot access with the same authenticity. Selection criteria prioritise football-first creators with engaged male 18-34 audiences.

### 7.5 Content and SEO - R126,000

| Element | Allocation | Deliverable |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| Always-On Education Hub | R60,000 | SEO-optimised landing pages: "What are fan tokens?", "How to buy crypto in SA", "Bitexen vs Luno", "PSL fan tokens explained". 12 cornerstone articles over 12 weeks. |

Content and SEO builds organic discoverability and positions Bitexen as the trusted authority on fan tokens in South Africa. This layer compounds over time and reduces paid acquisition costs.

### 7.6 Performance / App Install Campaigns - R126,000

## Key Notes and Assumptions

1. All pricing is sourced from vendor rate cards and proposals dated between July 2025 and March 2026. Rates are subject to change at vendor discretion.

2. DStv pricing reflects crypto-specific proposal and March 2026 rate card. Savings of up to 86% off standard rate card are built into the Young at Heart and On-Target packages.

3. Radio rates are based on 30-second commercial durations. Duration adjustments (15s, 45s, 60s) use standard conversion multipliers provided in the Mediamark rate card.

4. OnsideZA pricing excludes VAT. A multi-episode discount is expected but not yet confirmed.

5. SABC+ pricing is estimated based on Mediamark Digital video CPMs, as SABC+ does not publish a standalone rate card.

6. Polygon OOH pricing is estimated pending formal rate card submission. Figures will be updated upon receipt.

7. All vendor placements will be subject to standard terms and conditions as published in their respective rate cards.

8. Campaign timing is flexible across the 12-week launch window, with specific flight dates to be confirmed during the detailed media scheduling phase.
