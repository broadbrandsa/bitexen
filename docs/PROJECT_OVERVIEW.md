# PROJECT_OVERVIEW.md

## What This Site Is

A standalone, public-facing proposal website presenting Broadbrand's agency pitch to Bitexen for the launch of Bitexen in the South African market. The site translates the full written proposal into a structured, interactive web experience designed to be shared with Bitexen stakeholders directly via URL.

## Purpose

- Present the Broadbrand × Bitexen SA Launch Proposal in a polished, navigable format
- Communicate the "The 12th Man" campaign concept and its rationale
- Summarise the full media plan (R6,024,430 total budget) across ATL and digital channels
- Demonstrate Broadbrand's strategic, creative, and technical capabilities
- Support the sales conversation with a living document (updateable without reprinting)

## Target Audience

- Bitexen leadership team (Turkey HQ and any SA-facing stakeholders)
- Decision-makers evaluating agency proposals for the SA market entry

## Deployment Environment

- Hosted on Vercel
- Public URL (no authentication required)
- Optimised for desktop and mobile viewing
- Repository: https://github.com/broadbrandsa/bitexen
