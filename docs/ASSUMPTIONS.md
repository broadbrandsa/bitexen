# ASSUMPTIONS.md

## Scope Assumptions

1. **Directional strategy only.** All figures, targets, and media recommendations in this proposal are directional. Final numbers are subject to negotiation, vendor confirmation, and Bitexen's internal approval processes.

2. **No internal data access.** This proposal was developed without access to Bitexen's internal analytics, user data, or proprietary platform metrics. All performance benchmarks are derived from publicly available industry sources and comparable campaign data.

3. **FSCA license dependency.** Digital advertising on Google, Meta, and TikTok in South Africa requires Bitexen to hold an active FSCA CASP/FSP license. The campaign timeline assumes this license will be in place before launch. If the license is delayed, the campaign timeline and media mix will need to be revised.

4. **Football club partnership.** The fan token strategy assumes a PSL club partnership has been identified (as noted in the proposal). Specific club name and commercial terms are not disclosed in this proposal document.

5. **Budget is exclusive of VAT and management fees.** The R6,024,430 total budget excludes VAT on all line items. A 16% media management fee applies to the R4,514,430 working media component and is treated separately.

6. **Vendor pricing subject to change.** All rate cards referenced in the proposal are dated between July 2025 and March 2026. Pricing is subject to vendor change at any time. Formal quotations will be obtained before final budget sign-off.

7. **Bitexen app quality assumed.** App retention and conversion targets assume the Bitexen SA app provides a competitive onboarding experience (KYC flow, deposit mechanism, fan token interface). Poor UX will materially impact funnel conversion regardless of media investment.

8. **Requires validation post-approval.** All audience sizing, impression estimates, and conversion projections require formal validation against Bitexen's SA-specific data once the campaign is live. This proposal should be treated as a launch brief, not a guaranteed outcome document.

9. **DSG ecosystem integration is optional.** References to eInsurer, Digital Mobile MVNO, and CXG are strategic opportunities within the broader Digital Solutions Group. They are not pre-committed deliverables of this proposal.
